# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

This is a humanitarian project. We ask all contributors to approach their work and interactions with empathy, compassion, and respect for the people this software is meant to help.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in all communications
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Accept feedback gracefully and give it kindly
- Focus on what is best for the community and the people we serve

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional humanitarian context

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for repeated or severe violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening a private issue or contacting a project maintainer directly.
